About Blessed Maximum Care

We are a Washington‑licensed Adult Family Home serving adults who benefit from a small, home‑like environment with individualized attention. Our experienced caregivers—with RN oversight—partner with families and providers to deliver consistent, trauma‑informed care.

  • Person‑centered care planning and respectful ADL support
  • Medication administration & health coordination
  • Safe, predictable routines and de‑escalation strategies
  • Engaging activities: music, gardening, puzzles, light exercise

Who We Serve

Adults needing assistance with ADLs, memory support, diabetes management, mobility assistance, TBI and behavioral health supports.

Payer Sources

Private pay and WA State programs (ALTSA/Managed Care). We coordinate with case managers and guardians.

Quality & Compliance

Operated in alignment with WAC 388‑76 requirements for Adult Family Homes, including staffing, training, service delivery, resident rights, and safety.

Our Services

Medication & Health Coordination

Medication administration, pharmacy coordination, appointment scheduling, vitals monitoring, and provider communication.

ADLs & Personal Care

Bathing, grooming, dressing, toileting, mobility, and continence care—always with respect and privacy.

24/7 Supervision & Safety

Awake overnight staff, elopement prevention, fall‑risk reduction, and emergency response readiness.

Behavioral & Cognitive Support

Trauma‑informed care, de‑escalation, consistent routines, and collaborative goal setting.

Meaningful Activities

Music, puzzles, gardening, light exercise, and supported community outings.

Nutrition & Home Comforts

Balanced, culturally mindful meals and snacks; cozy private rooms; housekeeping and laundry.
